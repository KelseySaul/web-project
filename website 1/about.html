ABOUT US

We’ve been dedicated gamers for years, but really started being serious when we founded AiresHoops Basketball. Since then, we’ve welcomed an impressive group of talented and dedicated players into our basketball academy. Our growing popularity stems from our level of commitment to develop an all round player on-court and off the court and our dedication to success. All the programmes we have wouldn’t be possible without our supportive parents and committed players, who are always there for Fundamentor Kenya.

Through a large variety of drills and skills-specific trainings, FKBA aims to assist our trainees in becoming international players and perform to the best of their abililities. The principles of teamwork, discipline, dedication, and sportsmanship are instilled in them through the trainings. These are not only applicable on the basketball court, but also in life.
